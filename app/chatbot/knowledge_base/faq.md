## Resume Upload & Parsing

### What resume formats are supported?
PDF and DOCX (Microsoft Word) files. Maximum file size: 10MB.

### Why did resume parsing fail?
Common reasons:
- File is an image or scanned PDF (text must be selectable)
- File is corrupted
- Unsupported format (.pages, .odt, .txt)
- Special characters in filename

### Can I upload multiple resumes?
You can upload new resumes, but only one master resume is active at a time.

### How accurate is the resume parser?
Very accurate for standard formats.  If something is wrong, you can manually edit all fields.

### Does it work with non-traditional resume formats?
Yes, but simpler formats parse more accurately. Highly designed resumes may need manual corrections.

## Resume Tailoring

### How does AI tailoring work?
AI compares your resume with job description, identifies key requirements, prioritizes relevant experience, optimizes keywords, and generates a customized version.

### How long does tailoring take?
Typically 30-60 seconds per resume.

### Can I tailor one resume for multiple jobs?
Yes! Create a new application for each job. Each gets its own tailored version.

### Will it change my original resume?
No, your master resume stays unchanged. Each tailored version is separate.

### Can I edit the AI-generated resume?
Yes, all fields are editable. Make changes directly in the preview or edit mode.

### What if I don't like the AI suggestions?
You have full control - edit, delete, or regenerate any section.

## Keywords & ATS

### What are keywords?
Important terms from job descriptions that ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems) scan for.

### How do I see my keyword match?
View the "Keywords" section in any application. Green = in your resume, Red = missing.

### Should I add all missing keywords?
Only add keywords that are genuinely relevant to your experience. Don't keyword-stuff.

### What's a good keyword match percentage?
70-80% is excellent. Above 60% is good. Quality matters more than quantity.

### How can I improve my ATS score?
- Use exact terms from JD (not synonyms)
- Include skills in context, not just lists
- Use standard section headings
- Avoid tables, columns, images
- Use standard fonts

## Best Practices

### How often should I update my master resume?
Update whenever you gain new experience, skills, or certifications. Keep it current.

### Should I customize every application?
Yes! Generic resumes get 50% fewer responses. Always tailor.

### How many jobs should I apply to per day?
Quality over quantity. 3-5 well-researched, tailored applications better than 20 generic ones.

### When should I apply after a job is posted?
Within 48 hours if possible. First 100 applications get most attention.

### Should I always use AI suggestions?
AI provides a strong starting point, but add your personal touch. Review and refine all outputs.
